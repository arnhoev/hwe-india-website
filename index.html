Our Legacy

Pioneering Water Engineering Since 1959

Hindustan Water Engineering Company began its journey in 1959, manufacturing Pipe Drilling and Tapping Machines, Pipe Cutting Machines and Under Pressure Boring Machines. In the following decade, we expanded into state-of-the-art Water Meters for bulk flow measurement.

Today, HWE holds the distinction of manufacturing the largest sized Water Meters in India. With over six decades of unwavering commitment to the water industry, we continue to innovate with cost-efficient, reliable solutions that serve communities and industries across the nation.
